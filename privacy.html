Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Multi Post ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices regarding our application and website.

1. Information We Process

Our application operates on a "Privacy by Design" principle. We do not maintain a centralized database of your personal content.

  • Authentication Data: We use official OAuth tokens from Meta, Google, and TikTok to manage your sessions. These are stored securely in your browser/local environment.
  • Metadata: Social media titles, tags, and descriptions are processed during your active session to facilitate multi-platform posting.

2. Data Usage and Third-Party API Services

Multi Post uses API Services from Facebook, YouTube, and TikTok. By using this app, you agree to be bound by the respective Terms of Service of these platforms.

  • Meta: We access public profile data only when explicitly authorized to facilitate Facebook/Instagram posting.
  • Google/YouTube: We comply with the YouTube API Services Developer Policies.
  • TikTok: We use the TikTok for Business API to upload and manage content as directed by you.

3. Data Retention and Security

We do not store your videos on our servers. Metadata is retained only for the duration of the posting process. We implement industry-standard encryption for all data in transit between your device and the social media APIs.

4. User Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Under international privacy laws, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data. Since we do not store personal profiles, these rights are primarily exercised by revoking app permissions within your social media account settings.

5. User Data Deletion Instructions

To request the deletion of any transient data or to exercise your right to be forgotten regarding our app's integration:

  1. Send an email to alex@bryantgroupholdings.co.uk with the subject "Data Deletion Request."
  2. Log into your Facebook Account, navigate to "Settings & Privacy" > "Settings" > "Apps and Websites," find Multi Post, and click "Remove."
  3. Once removed, we lose all access to your tokens, effectively deleting your connection to our service.

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Significant changes will be notified via a notice on our primary website domain.
