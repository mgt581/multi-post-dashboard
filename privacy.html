Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use our application. Our app is designed to assist users with content optimization for social media platforms like YouTube and TikTok.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. Currently, our application does not store your personal data, videos, or login credentials on our servers.

  • Local Data: Any SEO titles or descriptions generated are stored locally on your device or handled during your active session.
  • Platform Data: When you use our "Copy to TikTok" features, we do not access your private TikTok account data unless explicitly authorized via their official login portal.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information processed by the app is used solely to:

  • Facilitate the transfer of SEO metadata (Titles and Descriptions) to social media upload pages.
  • Improve user workflow for content creators.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app interacts with third-party platforms. Please note that their use of your information is governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Google/YouTube Privacy Policy

4. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to ensure the integrity of the application. Since we do not host a centralized database of user content, the risk of data breach is significantly minimized.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
